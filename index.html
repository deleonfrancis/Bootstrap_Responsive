About Me

Hello, I’m Xavier. I believe greatness comes from within, and every living person can experience fullness of life. I was born in May Pen Clarendon, Jamaica, and moved to the United States at twelve years old. In 2004 I graduated from Edgewater H.S. in Orlando, Florida and went on to college to study Aeronautical Engineering. Instead of finishing that degree, I went a different route and obtain a certificate from the Federal Aviation Administration to be a certified Airframe & Powerplant Mechanic. After working for various companies and getting financial downturn of the aviation industry in 2020, I decided to pursue something that gave me great interest and that could fulfill my creating personality. This soul searching and self analyst led me to Web Development.


I’m currently a student again! Learning everything I can about this field in my course at the UCF Coding Bootcamp. It’s been challenging, but I’m enjoying the ride. I hope to one day partner with a company to gain some experience, so that one day I can be an entrepreneur. Time to tap into that greatness! Thanks for visiting my website.
